Ceramics: a business venture

Pottery by Luli is a creative venture founded by Lucia Ortiz Baeza in 2022. With a passion for craftsmanship and artistic expression, Lucia seeks to captivate her audience by sharing the intricate process behind each of her unique ceramic pieces. Her work is a celebration of color and form, where countless hues and shapes come together to create visually stunning and one-of-a-kind items. Through her Instagram page, Lucia invites followers to explore the beauty and artistry of ceramics, offering a window into the meticulous work that goes into every piece she creates.
